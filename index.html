Student Stories

Real experiences from students who have faced mental health challenges and found support.

During my sophomore year, I hit a really low point. The pressure of classes, work, and social life became overwhelming. Reaching out to campus counseling was the best decision I made. They helped me develop coping strategies and connected me with resources I didn't know existed. Now I'm in my senior year and doing so much better.

Jamie L.

Senior, Computer Science

I always thought I had to handle everything on my own. When my anxiety started affecting my sleep and my grades, I knew I needed help. MindSpace pointed me towards an online therapy platform that fit my schedule and budget. It's been a game-changer for me. I'm learning to manage my anxiety and feel more in control.

Sarah M.

Junior, Psychology

Joining a peer support group was initially intimidating, but it turned out to be incredibly helpful. Sharing my experiences with other students who understood what I was going through made me feel less isolated. We share tips, encourage each other, and celebrate small victories. It's like having a family away from home.

Carlos P.

Freshman, Engineering
