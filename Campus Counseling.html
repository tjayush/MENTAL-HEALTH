Counseling Sections

We offer a range of counseling services to support your well-being:

  • Individual Counseling: One-on-one sessions to address personal challenges.
  • Group Counseling: Connect with peers and share experiences in a supportive environment.
  • Career Counseling: Guidance and support for career exploration and planning.
  • Academic Counseling: Strategies for academic success and overcoming learning difficulties.
  • Mental Health Workshops: Informative sessions on various mental health topics.

About Our Services

The Campus Counseling service is committed to providing a safe, confidential, and supportive space for students to address their personal, academic, and career-related concerns. Our team of qualified and experienced counselors is dedicated to helping you navigate challenges, develop coping mechanisms, and enhance your overall well-being.

We believe that seeking support is a sign of strength, and we encourage all students to utilize our services whenever needed. Your privacy is our utmost priority, and all sessions are strictly confidential.

How to Enroll

Follow these simple steps to enroll in our counseling services:

  1. Review our services: Familiarize yourself with the different counseling sections we offer.
  2. Complete the enrollment form: Fill out the online form with your details and preferences.
  3. Submit the form: Once you have completed the form, click the submit button.
  4. Confirmation: You will receive a confirmation email upon successful submission.
  5. Schedule an initial consultation: Our team will contact you to schedule your first appointment.
